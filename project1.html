Craft Beer Can Design 

The goal is to create a can design that will create an interest and quirky worldany consumer passing by wants the chance to explore. This is achieved by using animals such as a bear,fox, and rabbit, that are each animal dawning piece’s of formal wear that fits the stereotypical view of each animal’s personality.As for the name of these exciting designs, it needed a name that had a meaning that represented all animals..."Animalia". In addition, the type of beer has been paired with a word that correlates to the animal, such as “Grizzly Dark Stout” and “Jack Rabbit Hop”. In order to continue the vintage formal aspect of the can design,a simple monogram H&S representing the Brewing Company "Hill and Sons" has been placed in a detailed Gold frame.
