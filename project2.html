Resturant Design 

Ashberry High Tea is an elegant, and peacful high tea resturant set in a bright solarium. A simple logo done in the design of ashberry’s was used to help carry through to clients the feel of Ashberry hightea. The colour is bright in order to translate the location of the resturant being set in a bright colourful room, also uses a shade of orange and red that ashberrys are usually found with. The type is a Mono type face to keep the logo smooth and concistant throughout and not to become to overbearing.
